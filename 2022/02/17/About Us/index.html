关于火烧艺术社 About Us

About Huoshao Art 关于火烧艺术社

大家好，这里是火烧艺术社。
Hello, we are Huoshao Art Club.

为什么是“火烧”？
Why “Huoshao”?

你可以从任何角度理解“火烧”，它放在标题中就已经具有了令我满意的张力。
You can interpret “Huoshao” from any angle, by googling it or asking your Chinese friends, and it has a satisfying tension just in the title.

为什么是“艺术社”？
Why “Art Club”?

我们搞不清自己要什么，但我们有将自己燃烧的冲动。
We can’t figure out what we want, but we have the urge to set ourselves on fire.

我们所有意图的指向都十分的幼稚，因此我们只能在自己作品的构成中传达超出自我的感觉。
All our intentions are very naive, so we can only convey a feeling beyond ourselves in the composition of our works.

我们不想奠定什么，但我们反对虚无主义——
We don’t want to lay anything down, but we are against nihilism-

艺术和文化早在100年前就分道扬镳，但仍然有商人们想让人民购买艺术。
Art and culture went their separate ways 100 years ago, but there are still businessmen who want the people to buy art.

不！这绝不是艺术，我们要反抗！
No! This is never ART, we are going to resist!

最后，引用1914年，拉里昂诺夫跟俄罗斯艺术杂志《戏剧与漫画》编辑的对话：
Finally, to quote a conversation between Mikhail Larionov and an editor in 1914 to end this introduction

——你们是未来主义者吗？
——是，我们是未来主义者。

——你们反对未来主义吗？
——对，我们反对未来主义，它应当在地球上消失。

——但是你们这么说不是自相矛盾吗？
——是的，我们这个流派的意图就是自相矛盾。

——你们是骗子吗？
——对，我们就是骗子。

——你们是一群平庸之辈吗？ 
——对，我们是一群平庸之辈。

——跟你们无法交谈吗？
——是的，无法交谈。

——你们这一年的愿望是什么？ 
——忠实于自己。

- Are you futurists?
- Yes, we are futurists.

- Are you against futurism?
- Yes, we are against futurism, it should disappear from the earth.

- But don't you contradict yourselves by saying that?
- Yes, the intention of our school is to contradict itself.

- Are you liars?
- Yes, we are liars.

- Are you a bunch of mediocrities? 
- Yes, we are a bunch of mediocrities.

- Is it impossible to talk to you?
- Yes, we can't talk.

- What is your wish for this year? 
- To be true to ourselves.
